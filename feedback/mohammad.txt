- The assumptions $\mathsf{AOMDL}$ or $\mathsf{OMDL}$ should be parameterized as $\ell$-$\mathsf{AOMDL}$. In Figure 2.1, you refer to $\ell$-$\mathsf{AOMDL}$, but otherwise the parameter $\ell$ appears without introduction. Making this explicit improves clarity. For example, in Theorem 3, instead of stating

 ``there exists a PPT adversary $\mathcal{D}$ that wins the $\mathsf{AOMDL}$ game,''

 you can then write

 ``there exists a PPT adversary $\mathcal{D}$ that wins the $\ell$-$\mathsf{AOMDL}$ game,''

 where $\ell$ is specified concretely in terms of the other parameters.

If giving a concrete bound is too cumbersome, it suffices to write ``for some polynomial $\ell$.'' :)
